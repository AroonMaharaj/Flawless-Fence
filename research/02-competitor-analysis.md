## Competitive Patterns — What the Top 10% Do

### 1. Individual Service Pages (Not Just "Residential" and "Commercial")
GTA Fencing has 10 service pages. The Fence Builder has 4+. Flawless Fence only has 2.
**Recommendation:** Create pages for Wood Fencing, Vinyl Fencing, Chain Link Fencing, Iron/Ornamental Fencing, Gate Installation, Fence Repair.

### 2. Location/Service Area Pages
GTA Fencing has 10 location pages. This is the #1 local SEO strategy.
**Recommendation:** Create a Service Areas page listing all served cities with local content.

### 3. Trust Signals & Stats
Every top site has: years in business, projects completed, customer rating, licensed/insured badges.
**Recommendation:** Add stats bar: "10+ Years" / "Hundreds of Projects" / "Licensed & Insured" / "Free Estimates"

### 4. Testimonials With Attribution
Top sites show name, location, and project type for each testimonial.
**Recommendation:** Extract real testimonials from Google reviews, attribute properly.

### 5. FAQ Section
GTA Fencing has 8 FAQs. This reduces friction and improves SEO.
**Recommendation:** Add FAQ covering permits, timeline, materials, pricing, warranty.

### 6. Warm, Earthy Palettes Beat Harsh Colors
GTA Fencing (warm cream + burnt orange) outperforms the harsh red/black sites.
Bramalea's deep purple is sophisticated. Cero's navy is professional.
**Recommendation:** Evolve Flawless Fence's #E21212 harsh red into a warmer, more sophisticated palette.

### 7. Photo Galleries Organized by Type
Cero organizes by wood/iron/chain link. This helps users self-select.
**Recommendation:** Gallery page with filterable categories.

### 8. Free Estimate as Primary CTA
100% of competitors use "Free Estimate" or "Free Quote" as the primary CTA.
**Recommendation:** Make "Get Your Free Estimate" the hero CTA, not just "Contact Us."

## What Bottom Competitors Do Wrong (AVOID)

1. **Template/placeholder content** — Urban Fence and several others have generic text
2. **Generic system fonts** — Arial/Helvetica feels cheap
3. **No stats or trust badges** — Missing social proof
4. **No testimonials** — Zero customer voice
5. **No FAQ** — Missed SEO and conversion opportunity
6. **Only 2 service pages** — "Residential" and "Commercial" is too broad
7. **No location pages** — Missing local SEO entirely
8. **Dated Wix/WordPress templates** — Look like every other trades site
9. **No video content** — Missing engagement opportunity
10. **No pricing transparency** — No calculator or pricing hints
