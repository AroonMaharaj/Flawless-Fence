Chain Link Fence Installation

Reliable protection at an excellent value. Our chain link fences deliver strong, long-lasting security for residential and commercial properties.

Why Chain Link

Proven Protection for Every Property

Chain link remains the go-to choice for security-focused fencing across the GTA. It delivers unmatched durability and value for residential and commercial applications.

Cost-Effective Security

Chain link is the most affordable fencing material per linear foot, delivering strong perimeter security without breaking the budget on large properties.

Low Maintenance

Galvanized chain link requires virtually no maintenance. No painting, staining, or sealing. It stands strong against Ontario's weather year after year.

Visibility

The open weave design lets you see through the fence, making it ideal for monitoring children, pets, and property while maintaining a clear sightline.

Commercial Grade Available

We install heavy-duty chain link for commercial, industrial, and municipal applications. Higher gauges, barbed wire, and razor ribbon options available.

Materials

Built to Last Decades

We use only high-quality chain link materials that are designed to withstand the rigours of Canadian weather and provide long-term security for your property.

  • Galvanized Steel (Standard) — Hot-dipped galvanized coating provides superior corrosion resistance. The industry standard for durability and affordability in the Canadian market.
  • Vinyl-Coated — Available in black, green, and brown, vinyl-coated chain link adds colour and extra corrosion protection. Blends better with landscaping and looks less industrial.
  • Various Gauges and Heights — From lightweight residential 11-gauge to heavy commercial 6-gauge. Heights from 3ft garden borders to 12ft security perimeters.
Applications

Where We Install Chain Link

Chain link fencing is versatile enough for virtually any application. Here are the most common projects we complete across the GTA.

  • Residential Yards — Affordable backyard containment for children and pets. Available with privacy slats for added screening.
  • Commercial Perimeters — Secure your business property with heavy-gauge chain link and lockable gates. We serve warehouses, yards, and parking lots across Mississauga and the GTA.
  • Sports Facilities — Backstops, tennis courts, and athletic field enclosures built to professional standards.
  • Pool Enclosures — Code-compliant pool fencing that meets Ontario safety requirements with self-closing, self-latching gates.
  • Pet Containment — Keep your dogs safe with chain link kennels and yard enclosures built to prevent digging and climbing.

FAQ

Chain Link Fencing Questions

Can chain link fences be made private?

Yes, we can add privacy slats, mesh screening, or hedge integration to turn a standard chain link fence into a semi-private or fully private barrier. Privacy slats are available in multiple colours and are woven through the chain link mesh to block visibility while maintaining the fence's structural strength.

What height chain link do you install?

We install chain link fencing from 3 feet for garden borders up to 12 feet for commercial and industrial security applications. The most common residential height is 4 to 6 feet. For commercial projects, we also install barbed wire and razor ribbon extensions for added security.

How long does chain link fencing last?

Galvanized chain link fencing typically lasts 30 to 50 years with virtually no maintenance. Vinyl-coated chain link can last even longer as the PVC coating provides an additional layer of corrosion protection. Chain link is one of the longest-lasting fencing materials available.
