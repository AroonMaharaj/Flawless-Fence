Vinyl Fence Installation

Zero maintenance, maximum durability. Our vinyl fences stand up to Ontario's toughest weather without fading, warping, or rotting.

Why Vinyl Fencing

The Maintenance-Free Solution

Vinyl fencing has become one of the fastest-growing choices for GTA homeowners who want a beautiful fence without the upkeep of wood.

Zero Maintenance

No painting, staining, or sealing required. Vinyl fencing never needs the annual upkeep that wood demands, saving you time and money year after year.

Weather Resistant

Engineered to withstand Ontario's harsh freeze-thaw cycles, heavy snow loads, and intense summer UV without cracking, warping, or discolouring.

Long Lifespan

Quality vinyl fencing can last 30 years or more with virtually no maintenance. Many manufacturers offer lifetime warranties on their products.

Clean Modern Look

Vinyl fences maintain their crisp, clean appearance for decades. The colour is molded throughout the material, so it never peels, chips, or flakes.

Materials

Commercial-Grade Vinyl

We install only commercial-grade PVC vinyl fencing that meets the highest standards for impact resistance, UV stability, and long-term performance in Canadian climates.

  • Commercial-Grade PVC Vinyl — Thicker walls and reinforced rails provide superior strength compared to builder-grade products. Engineered for high winds and heavy snow loads.
  • UV-Stabilized — Advanced UV inhibitors are blended into the vinyl compound to prevent yellowing and fading, even after years of direct sun exposure.
  • Multiple Colours — Choose from white, almond, tan, grey, and woodgrain finishes to complement your home's exterior and landscape design.
Styles

Vinyl Fence Styles

We offer a full range of vinyl fence styles to match your property's needs, from full-height privacy to decorative borders.

  • Privacy — Solid tongue-and-groove panels provide complete privacy with a clean, seamless look. Available in 4ft to 8ft heights.
  • Semi-Privacy — Spaced boards allow air circulation and light while still providing a visual barrier. Ideal for pool areas and side yards.
  • Picket — Classic picket styling in a modern, maintenance-free material. Perfect for front yards and garden borders throughout the GTA.

FAQ

Vinyl Fencing Questions

Is vinyl fencing as strong as wood?

Yes, modern commercial-grade vinyl fencing is engineered for exceptional strength and flexibility. Unlike wood, vinyl won't crack or splinter in Ontario's cold winters. The material flexes under wind load rather than snapping, and internal aluminum reinforcements in posts and rails add structural integrity.

What colours are available?

We offer vinyl fencing in white, almond, tan, grey, and realistic woodgrain finishes. The colour is molded throughout the entire thickness of the material, so it won't peel, chip, or fade over time. We'll bring samples to your free consultation so you can see the colours against your home.

How do you clean a vinyl fence?

Simply rinse your vinyl fence with a garden hose to remove dirt and debris. For stubborn spots, a mild soap and water solution with a soft brush does the trick. No painting, staining, or sealing is ever required, making vinyl one of the lowest-maintenance fence options available.
