Our Services

Fence Repair Services

Don't replace your entire fence when a repair will do. We restore damaged, leaning, and weathered fences to like-new condition.

Why Repair

Save Money, Restore Your Fence

Not every damaged fence needs to be replaced. Our experienced team will assess the damage honestly and recommend the most cost-effective solution for your situation.

Save Money vs Replacement

Targeted repairs can cost a fraction of a full replacement. When the structure is sound, replacing damaged boards or posts saves you significant money.

Fast Turnaround

Most fence repairs can be completed in a single visit. We aim for same-week service and prioritize emergency storm damage repairs to restore your property's security quickly.

Expert Assessment

With over a decade of experience, we know when a fence can be saved and when replacement makes more sense. We'll give you an honest recommendation every time.

All Fence Types

We repair wood, vinyl, chain link, and ornamental fences. No matter what material your fence is made of, we have the skills and materials to fix it.

Common Repairs

What We Fix

Ontario's weather is tough on fences. High winds, ice storms, heavy snow, and freeze-thaw cycles take their toll. Here are the most common repairs we handle across the GTA.

  • Storm Damage Repair — Fallen trees, high winds, and ice can wreck fence sections overnight. We respond quickly to restore your perimeter and property security.
  • Leaning / Fallen Post Straightening — Posts lean when concrete footings crack or soil shifts. We reset or replace posts with new concrete below the frost line.
  • Rotting Board Replacement — Individual rotting boards can be replaced without removing the entire fence. We source matching lumber for a seamless repair.
  • Gate Realignment & Hardware — Sagging, sticking, or broken gates are one of the most common fence issues. We realign frames, replace hinges, and install new latches.
  • Chain Link Repair & Re-tensioning — Stretched, torn, or sagging chain link mesh can be patched, replaced, or re-tensioned to restore a tight, secure perimeter.
  • Post Replacement (4x4 or 6x6) — When posts are rotted at ground level, we excavate and replace them with new pressure-treated posts set in concrete.

FAQ

Fence Repair Questions

How do I know if my fence needs repair or replacement?

As a general rule, if more than 50% of the boards or posts are damaged, replacement is usually more cost-effective than repairing individual sections. If the damage is localized to a few boards, a single post, or a gate, repair is typically the better option. We'll give you an honest assessment during our free visit and explain the pros and cons of each approach for your specific situation.

Can you match my existing fence style?

Yes, we source matching materials and replicate the original design so repairs blend seamlessly with the rest of your fence. For wood fences, we match the board width, thickness, and style. For vinyl, chain link, and ornamental fences, we source components from the same or compatible manufacturers. In most cases, repaired sections are virtually indistinguishable from the original.

Do you offer emergency fence repair?

Yes, we prioritize storm damage and security-related repairs. If a fallen tree or severe weather has compromised your fence and your property's security is at risk, call us as soon as possible. We'll respond quickly to assess the damage and can often perform temporary securing the same day, with full repairs following shortly after.
