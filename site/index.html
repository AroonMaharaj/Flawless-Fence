FAQ

Common Questions About Fencing

How long does a typical fence installation take?

Most residential installations take 1-3 days depending on the size and terrain. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your free consultation.

Do I need a permit for my fence?

Permit requirements vary by municipality across the GTA. Our team is familiar with local regulations in Mississauga, Toronto, Oakville, Brampton, and Milton, and we'll help you navigate the permit process.

What fence material is best for Ontario weather?

We recommend pressure-treated wood or cedar for natural beauty, vinyl for low maintenance, and galvanized chain link for durability. We'll help you choose based on your priorities and budget.

How much does a new fence cost?

Pricing depends on material, fence height, property size, and terrain. We provide free, no-obligation estimates with transparent pricing — no hidden fees.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes, we stand behind every fence we build. We provide a workmanship warranty and use materials with manufacturer warranties. Details are discussed during your consultation.

Can you repair my existing fence instead of replacing it?

Absolutely. We assess the damage and recommend repair when it makes sense. We fix storm damage, replace rotting boards, straighten leaning posts, and more.
