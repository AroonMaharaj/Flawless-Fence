Why Ornamental Fencing

Ornamental iron and aluminum fencing delivers a level of sophistication that no other material can match, while providing robust security for your property.

Materials

Iron & Aluminum Options

We work with the finest ornamental fencing materials to deliver a product that matches the prestige of your property and stands the test of Ontario's demanding climate.

  • Wrought Iron — The classic choice for ornamental fencing. Wrought iron can be custom-forged into virtually any design, from traditional to contemporary. Heavy, strong, and endlessly customizable.
  • Aluminum — A lightweight, rust-proof alternative that closely mimics the look of wrought iron at a more accessible price point. Ideal for pool enclosures and coastal-adjacent properties.
  • Powder-Coated Finishes — All our ornamental fences are finished with industrial-grade powder coating in black, bronze, or white for long-lasting colour and corrosion resistance.
Styles

Design Options

From classic Victorian-inspired designs to clean modern lines, we offer ornamental fence styles that complement any architectural style.

  • Traditional Picket — Evenly spaced vertical pickets with decorative finials create a timeless look that suits heritage and traditional homes throughout the GTA.
  • Spear Top — Pointed picket tops provide an elegant security feature. The most popular choice for front yard perimeters and estate entrances.
  • Flat Top — Clean, modern horizontal top rail with uniform pickets. A contemporary option that pairs well with modern architecture and commercial properties.
  • Custom Scrollwork — Decorative scrolls, rings, and custom forged elements add unique character. We can replicate existing designs or create something entirely new.

FAQ

Ornamental Fencing Questions

Does iron fencing rust?

Our iron fences are galvanized and finished with industrial-grade powder coating that provides excellent rust resistance. With this dual protection, iron fences perform well in Ontario's climate for many years. We also offer aluminum alternatives that are completely rust-proof, making them ideal for pool areas and properties near salt-treated roads.

Can ornamental fences be used for pool enclosures?

Yes, we install code-compliant ornamental pool fences that meet Ontario safety requirements. Pool fences must have specific picket spacing, height, and self-closing, self-latching gates. Aluminum is particularly popular for pool fencing because it is rust-proof and handles the moisture and chemical exposure of pool environments.

What's the difference between wrought iron and aluminum?

Wrought iron is heavier, stronger, and can be custom-forged into intricate designs. It is the premium choice for estates and heritage properties but requires periodic maintenance to prevent rust. Aluminum is significantly lighter, completely rust-proof, and more affordable while closely mimicking the look of iron. For most residential applications, aluminum provides the best balance of aesthetics, durability, and value.
