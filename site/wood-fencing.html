Wood Fence Installation

Natural beauty, lasting strength. Our pressure-treated and cedar wood fences bring warmth and privacy to your property.

Why Wood Fencing

The Natural Choice for Your Property

Wood fencing remains the most popular choice across the GTA for good reason. It delivers privacy, beauty, and value that other materials simply can't match.

Privacy & Security

A solid wood privacy fence creates a complete visual barrier, giving you a secure, private backyard for your family to enjoy year-round.

Natural Beauty

Nothing matches the warmth of real wood. Cedar and pressure-treated lumber offer rich textures and natural grain patterns that enhance any landscape.

Property Value

A well-built wood fence is one of the highest-return improvements you can make to your home, boosting curb appeal and resale value across the GTA market.

Customizable Designs

Wood can be cut, shaped, stained, and painted to match any vision. From modern horizontal slat to classic picket, the design possibilities are endless.

Materials

Premium Wood Options

We use only the highest-grade lumber for every wood fence installation. Each material is selected for its ability to withstand Ontario's freeze-thaw cycles, heavy snowfall, and summer heat.

  • Pressure-Treated Lumber — The most affordable and durable option. Chemically treated to resist rot, insects, and fungal decay. Ideal for budget-conscious homeowners who want long-lasting performance.
  • Western Red Cedar — Naturally rot-resistant with a beautiful, rich grain. Cedar contains natural oils that repel insects without chemical treatment. Ages to a gorgeous silver-grey patina or can be stained to maintain its warm tones.
  • Custom Heights — We install wood fences in 4ft, 5ft, 6ft, and 8ft heights to suit your needs, from decorative picket borders to full-height privacy screens.
Styles

Fence Styles We Build

Every property is different, and we offer a range of wood fence styles to complement your home's architecture and meet your specific needs.

  • Privacy Fence (Board-on-Board) — Overlapping boards create zero gaps for complete privacy. The most popular choice for backyard fences across Mississauga and the GTA.
  • Shadowbox — Alternating boards on each side of the rail create a semi-private fence with a finished look from both sides. Great for neighbourly properties.
  • Lattice Top — A solid privacy fence base with a decorative lattice section on top. Adds elegance while allowing light and airflow.
  • Post and Rail — An open, rustic design ideal for property boundaries, gardens, and rural properties. Available in 2-rail and 3-rail configurations.

FAQ

Wood Fencing Questions

How long does a wood fence last in Ontario?

A well-built wood fence typically lasts 15 to 25 years in Ontario's climate. Cedar fences tend to last on the longer end of that range due to their natural rot resistance. Regular maintenance such as staining or sealing every few years can extend the life of your fence significantly.

Do you use concrete for fence posts?

Yes, all of our fence posts are set in concrete below the frost line. In Ontario, the frost line is approximately 4 feet deep, and we dig our post holes accordingly. This prevents frost heave from shifting your posts and keeps your fence straight and stable for years.

Can you build a fence on uneven ground?

Yes, we specialize in stepped and racked installations for sloped properties. Stepped fencing follows the slope in uniform sections, while racked fencing angles each panel to match the grade. We'll assess your terrain during the free estimate and recommend the best approach for a clean, professional result.
