Our Services

Gate Installation

The perfect finishing touch for your fence. We design and install custom gates that combine function, security, and style.

Why a Custom Gate

Complete Your Fence with the Right Gate

A gate is more than an opening in your fence. It is the first impression visitors get, the access point you use every day, and a critical part of your property's security.

Convenience & Access Control

Custom gates provide controlled access to your property, from simple latches to full automatic systems with remote, keypad, or smartphone operation.

Security

A properly installed gate with quality hardware is essential for property security. We install self-closing, self-latching gates for pool areas and lockable gates for driveways.

Curb Appeal

A beautifully crafted gate makes a statement. From arched wood driveway gates to sleek iron pedestrian gates, the right gate transforms your property's entrance.

Custom Sizing

Every gate is built to fit your exact opening. From narrow pedestrian gates to wide double driveway gates, we custom-size every installation.

Gate Types

Gates for Every Application

We install gates to complement any fence style and serve any purpose, from simple garden access to fully automated driveway entrances.

  • Single Swing Gates — The most common gate type for pedestrian access. Hinges on one side and swings inward or outward. Available in all fence materials.
  • Double Swing / Driveway Gates — Two gate panels that meet in the middle for wide vehicle access. Standard widths from 10 to 16 feet for cars, trucks, and equipment.
  • Sliding Gates — Ideal for driveways with limited swing clearance. The gate slides along a track parallel to the fence line. Perfect for sloped driveways.
  • Pedestrian Gates — Compact, single-panel gates for walkways and side yards. Self-closing and self-latching options for pool code compliance.
  • Automatic Gate Operators — We install automatic operators for swing and sliding gates with remote control, keypad, intercom, or smartphone access.
Materials

Built to Match Your Fence

We build gates in every material we install, ensuring a seamless match with your existing or new fence.

  • Wood — Pressure-treated and cedar gates to match your wood fence. Available in privacy, lattice-top, and custom designs with heavy-duty hardware.
  • Vinyl — Maintenance-free vinyl gates that match our vinyl fence systems. Reinforced with aluminum inserts for strength and longevity.
  • Chain Link — Durable chain link gates for residential and commercial applications. Available in standard galvanized or vinyl-coated finishes.
  • Ornamental Iron & Aluminum — Elegant ornamental gates with custom designs, scrollwork, and decorative elements. The premium choice for estate entrances.

FAQ

Gate Installation Questions

Can you add a gate to an existing fence?

Absolutely. We can match your existing fence style and material for a seamless addition. We'll reinforce the gate posts with concrete footings and install heavy-duty hardware to ensure the gate operates smoothly and lasts as long as your fence. During the free estimate, we'll assess the best location and size for your new gate.

Do you install automatic gate openers?

Yes, we install automatic operators for both swing and sliding gates. Options include remote control, wireless keypad, intercom systems, and smartphone-connected operators that let you open your gate from anywhere. We'll recommend the right operator based on your gate size, weight, and usage frequency.

What width gate do I need for my driveway?

Standard driveway gates are 10 to 16 feet wide, which comfortably accommodates cars, SUVs, and pickup trucks. For properties that need to accommodate larger vehicles, trailers, or equipment, we can build wider openings. We'll measure your driveway and recommend the right size during your free estimate to ensure comfortable access.
